# Privacy Policy — Learno

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Learno ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered educational application designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have as a parent or guardian.

This policy complies with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, and applicable local data protection laws.

---

## 2. Who This Policy Covers

This application is intended for children under 13, used under parental supervision. Parents or legal guardians must create accounts and provide consent before a child uses the app.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Account Information (Parent)

When a parent or guardian registers, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (stored as a secure, one-way hash — never readable)
- Parent name (optional)

### 3.2 Child Profile Information

When a parent creates a child profile, we collect:
- Child's first name or nickname (chosen by the parent)
- Child's age or grade level
- Preferred language (English or Arabic)

**We do not collect:** full name, date of birth, address, phone number, school name, or any government ID for the child.

### 3.3 Usage Data

While the child uses the app, we collect:
- Lesson progress and completion data
- Scores and achievement badges earned
- Voice input (audio) — processed in real time for speech recognition; **not stored or transmitted to third parties**
- Session duration and timestamps

### 3.4 Device Information

We may collect:
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Crash reports (anonymous, for debugging)

We **do not** collect device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID), location data, contacts, or photos.

## 4. How We Use the Information

| Purpose | Legal Basis |

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|------------|
| OpenAI (GPT-4o) | AI tutor responses | Lesson text prompts only (no personal identifying information) |

We do not share any personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or social networks.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on secure servers.
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry standard).
- All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
- We restrict access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
- We conduct regular security reviews.

We retain account data for as long as the account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

---

## 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take special measures to protect children's privacy:

- **No direct collection from children.** All account creation and consent is handled by the parent/guardian.
- **No behavioral advertising.** We never show ads to children or use their data to build advertising profiles.
- **No social features.** The app has no chat, forums, or public profiles.
- **Voice data is not retained.** Audio captured for speech recognition is processed in real time and immediately discarded.
- **Parental controls.** Parents can view, update, or delete their child's profile at any time from the Parent Dashboard.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

## 8. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about your child.
- **Correct** inaccurate information.
- **Delete** your child's profile and all associated data.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time by deleting the account.
- **Opt out** of any future data collection.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **privacy@learno.app** (replace with your real email before publishing).

We will respond within 30 days.
